==========< FEATURES >==========

 - Original sprite animation by Coelasquid
 - Coding and gameplay design by PotS (that's me)
 - 13 palettes
 - A.I.



==========< MOVELIST >==========

.Light Attack:         A or X
.Medium Attack:        B or Y
.Hard Attack:          C or Z
.Running Charge:       Tap forward twice
.Ram:                  Any button while running
.Back Dash:            Tap backward twice
.Taunt:                Start


Notes:
 - Can do ground chain combos with the light, medium and hard attacks
 - Standing light, medium and hard attack can cancel into Running Charge
 - Air Hard Attack can hit opponents that are on the floor
 - Has super armor against normal and special moves
 - Super armor gets progressively better as he gets struck by more consecutive hits
 - Can only be thrown by super throws
